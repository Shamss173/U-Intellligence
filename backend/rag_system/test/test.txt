This is a test document about the new Platinum Credit Card.
The Platinum Credit Card offers a 0% introductory APR for the first 15 months on balance transfers.
After the introductory period, a variable APR of 19.99% to 29.99% applies.
There is a $95 annual fee.
Cardholders earn 3x points on dining and travel purchases.
All other purchases earn 1x points.
This card is issued by the Example Bank.
To apply, visit our website or any local branch.
